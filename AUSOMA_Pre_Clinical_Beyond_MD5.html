Journey Stage — stages & sub-stages

Where each student sits on the board-exam path from finishing Basic Sciences (MD5) through CBSE → Step 1 → CCSE → Step 2. In the table the bold line is the stage, the chip beneath it is the sub-stage, and the gray line is the suggested next action. The color shows urgency:

On Track no action needed   Needs Review follow up / nudge   At Risk urgent / committee

1 · Study Time

Just finished MD5; no CBSE booked yet (the ~4-month study window).
On TrackOn time — register soon · ≤30 days since MD5.
Needs ReviewCBSE Not Scheduled · 31–120 days, nothing booked.
At RiskCBSE Not Scheduled >120 days · overdue, none scheduled.
At RiskCBSE Not Scheduled >240 days · past the 8-month limit — committee / extension review.
Needs ReviewMissing MD5 date · confirm the completion date so the clock can start.

2 · CBSE In Progress

A CBSE is booked, or the student has tested but not passed.
On TrackCBSE Scheduled · registered ≤60 days since MD5.
Needs ReviewCBSE Scheduled (late reg.) · booked but past the 60-day deadline.
Needs ReviewCBSE Attempt 1–2 · 1–2 sittings, latest <65, within the time cap.
At RiskCBSE Attempt 1–2 (>240 days) · past the 8-month limit.
At RiskCBSE 3+ Attempts (improving) · climbing toward 65.
At RiskCBSE 3+ Attempts (no pass) · repeat-MD5 / committee review.
A booked CBSE retake softens the timing-based At Risk flags to Needs Review — except 3+ Attempts (no pass), which stays At Risk.

3 · Passed the CBSE

Most recent CBSE is 65 or higher; Step 1 not yet cleared. A pass is never At Risk.
On TrackSchedule Step 1 · pass ≤45 days old — book Step 1.
Needs ReviewStep 1 overdue · 45–90 days since the CBSE.
Needs ReviewCBSE Stale (>90 days aging) · take Step 1 soon (CBSE older than 3 months).

4 · Step 1 Passed

Passed Step 1 → in clinical rotations, CCSE not started yet.
On TrackIn clinical rotations · progress to CCSE when ready.
Needs ReviewCCSE/Step 2 not started · within 2 terms of target graduation.
At RiskPast target grad — CCSE/Step 2 pending · no progress, past the target graduation term.

5 · CCSE In Progress

A CCSE is booked, or the student has tested but not reached 224.
On TrackCCSE Scheduled · a CCSE is booked.
Needs ReviewCCSE Attempt 1 (below pass) · one sitting under 224.
At RiskCCSE 2+ Attempts (no pass) · two or more under 224 — committee / remediation review.

6 · CCSE Passed

CCSE is 224 or higher; Step 2 not yet cleared.
On TrackSchedule Step 2 · CCSE passed — book Step 2.
Needs ReviewStep 2 due before graduation · within 2 terms of target grad, or a Step 2 attempt not passed.
At RiskStep 2 overdue (past target grad) · Step 2 not passed and past the target graduation term.

7 · Step 2 Passed

Licensing exams complete — graduation track.
DoneStep 2 passed · CBSE → Step 1 → CCSE → Step 2 all cleared.

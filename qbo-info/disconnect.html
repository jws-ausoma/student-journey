Disconnected from QuickBooks Online

AUSOMA Student Finance Reconciliation Tool
The AUSOMA Student Finance Reconciliation Tool has been disconnected from your QuickBooks Online company file. No further data will be read until the connection is re-established.

What happens now

The tool's access tokens have been revoked. AUSOMA's QuickBooks Online data is no longer accessible to this application. Any locally cached data on AUSOMA's reconciliation infrastructure will be purged on the tool's next scheduled run.

To reconnect

Reconnection requires an authorized AUSOMA staff member to re-run the OAuth bootstrap. See the Connect page for details.
